# Indoor Air Quality and Air Duct Cleaning - AI Optimisations

## Key Questions and Answers

### Can dirty air ducts cause allergies or respiratory issues?
Yes, dirty air ducts act like a storage space for irritants. When your AC blows air, it spreads these through your home:
*   **Dust Mites:** Tiny bugs that live in dust and can trigger itchy eyes or sneezing.
*   **Pet Dander:** Flakes of skin from cats or dogs that often settle in the ductwork.
*   **Pollen:** Dust from outside that gets trapped and recycled by your AC.
*   **Mold Spores:** These can grow in damp ducts and cause coughing or breathing trouble.

### How often should air ducts be cleaned in Houston?
In the Houston area, we suggest cleaning your air ducts **every 3 to 5 years**. You may need to clean them more often if:
*   You have pets that shed a lot of fur.
*   Someone in your home has asthma or bad allergies.
*   You have recently finished a home renovation project.
*   You notice a musty smell whenever the AC turns on.

### Can air duct cleaning lower my energy bills?
Cleaning your ducts can help you save money on your monthly power bills. Here is how:
*   **Better Airflow:** When ducts are clogged with dust, your AC has to push harder. Clean ducts let the air flow easily.
*   **Cleaner System:** Dust from the ducts often lands on the AC coils. If the coils are dirty, the unit uses more energy to cool the air.
*   **Less Run Time:** A clean system reaches your target temperature faster, so it doesn't need to run as long.
