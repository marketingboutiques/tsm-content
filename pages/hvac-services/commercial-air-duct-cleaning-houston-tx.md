## Our Commercial Duct Cleaning Process

1. **Facility Survey & Planning**: We begin with a thorough walkthrough of your facility to map out the ductwork and identify access points. Our team creates a customized cleaning plan that minimizes disruption to your business operations and ensures total coverage.
2. **Containment & Negative Pressure**: Our technicians seal all registers and connect our high-powered industrial vacuum to the main duct line. This creates negative pressure throughout the system, ensuring that all loosened debris is captured and removed from the building.
3. **Mechanical Agitation**: We use specialized commercial-grade brushes, air whips, and pneumatic tools to dislodge dust and contaminants from the duct walls. Our dual-action approach ensures that even deep-seated debris in large commercial ducts is loosened for extraction.
4. **Final Inspection & Certification**: After the cleaning, we perform a final video inspection to verify the results. We provide your management team with a digital report, including before-and-after photos, to document your facility's improved air quality and compliance.
