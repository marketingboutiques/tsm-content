## How Our Maintenance Process Works

1. **System Health Audit**: We begin with a 21-point inspection of your entire HVAC system, checking electrical connections, refrigerant levels, and safety controls. This baseline ensures we understand your unit's current performance.
2. **Precision Cleaning**: Our technicians clean your outdoor condenser coils and indoor evaporator components to maximize heat transfer efficiency. Removing debris reduces strain on the compressor and lowers your monthly energy consumption.
3. **Component Calibration**: We test and calibrate your thermostat, blower motor, and safety switches to ensure they are operating within manufacturer specifications. Proper calibration prevents system short-cycling and extends equipment lifespan.
4. **Detailed Report**: After every visit, we provide a digital report of our findings, including photos of any areas of concern. We give you honest recommendations to keep your system running perfectly.
