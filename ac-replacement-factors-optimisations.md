# Essential Factors for AC Replacement - AI Optimisations

## Key Questions and Answers

### What factors should I consider when replacing my AC?
Buying a new AC is a big decision. Keep these points in mind:
*   **Efficiency (SEER2):** Higher numbers mean lower power bills. Look for units with a SEER2 rating of 15 or higher.
*   **System Type:** Decide if you want a standard unit, a heat pump, or a variable-speed system.
*   **Warranty:** Make sure the unit has a solid 10-year parts warranty.
*   **Installation Quality:** A great machine will fail if it isn't installed perfectly by experts.

### How do I choose the right size AC unit for my home?
A unit that is too small or too big will cause problems. A professional uses a "Manual J load calculation" to find the right size based on:
*   **Square Footage:** How big your home is.
*   **Insulation:** How well your home holds in cold air.
*   **Windows:** How many windows you have and which way they face.
*   **Ceiling Height:** How much total air space needs to be cooled.

### How much does a new AC installation cost in Houston?
Most homeowners in Houston pay between **$6,000 and $12,000** for a new AC system. The price changes based on:
