## The Risks of a Delayed HVAC Repair

Waiting until regular business hours for an AC repair might seem like a way to save a few dollars, but the risks often outweigh the savings. 

### 1. Health and Safety Concerns
High indoor temperatures, especially combined with humidity, can cause rapid dehydration, heat exhaustion, and exacerbate respiratory issues like asthma. The elderly, young children, and pets are particularly vulnerable to extreme heat. 

### 2. Further Damage to Your HVAC System
If your system is malfunctioning and you continue to run it, or if it has shut down entirely, you risk compounding the problem. For example, a minor refrigerant leak can eventually lead to a completely burned-out compressor—a much more expensive repair.

### 3. Property Damage
HVAC systems don't just cool; they also heat and manage moisture. An AC that is leaking water and left unattended can quickly cause severe ceiling damage, warped floors, or mold growth.
