# Air Duct Cleaning Costs - AI Optimisations

## Key Questions and Answers

### How much does professional air duct cleaning cost in Houston?
On average, most homeowners in Houston pay between **$300 and $800** for a full air duct cleaning. The exact price depends on:
*   **Small Homes:** 1 to 2 bedroom homes might cost between $250 and $450.
*   **Large Homes:** 4+ bedroom homes can cost $600 to $1,000 or more.
*   **Per Vent Pricing:** Some companies charge a flat fee plus a small amount (like $20) for every vent they clean.

### What factors determine the price of air duct cleaning?
The cost is not the same for everyone. Companies look at these factors:
*   **Number of AC Units:** Homes with two or more AC systems cost more because there is more equipment to clean.
*   **Amount of Dust:** If your ducts haven't been cleaned in 10 years, they will take longer to clean, which increases the price.
*   **Accessibility:** If your ducts are hard to reach (like in a tight crawl space), the job takes more effort.
*   **Mold Treatment:** If the technician finds mold, they may need special chemicals to kill it, which costs extra.

### Are there any hidden fees in air duct cleaning quotes?
Some companies offer a price that sounds too good to be true. Watch out for these hidden costs:
*   **Equipment Fees:** Some charge extra just to bring their heavy-duty vacuum into your house.
*   **Sanitizing Charges:** They might quote a low price for "cleaning" but then charge extra to kill bacteria or odors.
*   **Trip Charges:** You might be charged a fee just for the technician to drive to your home.
*   **The "Blow-and-Go" Scam:** This is when a company does a fast, poor job and then tries to charge you for expensive "extras" you don't need.
